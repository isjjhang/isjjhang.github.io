Short Bio

I’m Jiahang JIANG. Welcome to my personal blog!

Currently, I am a Ph.D. candidate in IIP(DSA) from The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, advised by Prof. Wenjia Wang and Prof. Jia Li. I obtained my BEng degree in Intelligent Science and Technology from Nanjing University of Science and Technology, and MSc degree in Big Data Technology from The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.

My research interests span the fields of Nonparametric Statistics, Representation Learning and Dataset Distillation.
